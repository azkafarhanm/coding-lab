Chicken Satay Recipe

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Preparation
    • Gather ingredients
    • Prepare workspace
    • Check utensils
  2. Ingredients
    • Chicken thighs or breast
    • Wooden skewers
    • Peanut sauce
    • Turmeric powder
    • Garlic and spices
  3. Installation
    • Marinate chicken
    • Soak skewers
    • Prepare grill
  4. Practice
    • Thread chicken onto skewers
    • Grill until golden brown
    • Serve with peanut sauce

Important Notes

  • Always soak wooden skewers before using
  • Marinate chicken for at least 2 hours
  • Best served fresh off the grill
